Body Fat % Calculator

Body fat % shows how much of your body is fat compared to lean mass. This calculator uses the U.S. Navy Method to estimate it based on your measurements—helping you track fitness and health more accurately than just weight or BMI.

Categories

Category Men (%) Women (%)
Essential Fat 2–5% 10–13%
Athletes 6–13% 14–20%
Fitness 14–17% 21–24%
Average 18–24% 25–31%
Obese 25%+ 32%+
