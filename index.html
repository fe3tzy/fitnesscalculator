What Makes Up Your TDEE?

BMR

~60–70% of TDEE

Calories burned at rest for basic body functions (e.g., breathing, circulation).

TEF

~10% of TDEE

Calories burned to digest, absorb, and process food.

NEAT

~10–20% of TDEE

Calories burned during daily activities (walking, fidgeting, chores).

EAT

~5–15% of TDEE

Calories burned through intentional exercise or sports.
